Hyderabadi for the weekend

Immerse yourself in the unique experience that only the city of pearls can provide. With a weekend following, here are some amazing things you can do in the city.

Walk With Peacocks

Morning people can take a walk along the very famous KBR park and probably sight some peacocks in the early hours of the day. Recommended time to go would be around 5.30am.

Shop The Night Away

Take a trip to the old city on Saturday night, getting lost in the famous Laad bazaar, Madeena Market and many more of such markets, and explore the true roots of the Nizami culture. Best things you can get here are bangles, pearls, clothes and great street food! (Sunday is not recommended as most of the markets are shut).

Get Crafty

If your mood is to shop for things that celebrate the Telengana and Andhra spirit, visit the Shilparamam market, you get some great accessories and house decor stuff to flavor yourself with a little Hyderabadi. (The market shuts by 8.30pm)

Too Posh for the Streets

Not a fan of bargains and only enjoy the High brands? We've got you covered! You can always go over to Inorbit Mall or Forum Sujana. These places are home to your famous ones like, M&S, Aeropostale, Zara, Forever 21 and more.

Fancy a midnight Snack

If food is on your mind, we recommend you take a late night trip to our greatly enjoyed drive in restaurants around the city. Pick any one you love they are all good. You can also enjoy some great diverse food at Hitech City with a large number of food trucks and pop ups (Almost all of these food joints open after 8.30pm and run until 2am).

What Else?!

There is a lot more to do in this city and if you have time we recommend you visit the Charminar, enjoy a game of laser tag, visit Golkonda fort, Hussain Sagar lake, Taramati palace, Wonder la amusement park, runway 9 paint ball, go Go karting at the airport and these are just a few off the tip.
